Welcome to Our Company

Established in 2015, Constratech Trading is a fully registered construction company operating in Malawi. We are registered with the Registrar of Companies, the National Construction Industry Council of Malawi (NCIC), the Malawi Revenue Authority, the National Water Resources Authority Ministry, PPDA, and the Ministry of Trade.

Our NCIC registration qualifies us for projects up to MK 500 million in both Civil and Building categories, demonstrating our capacity to handle substantial construction projects.
