Welcome to Constratech Trading CMS

Manage all your website content from this central dashboard. Select a section from the sidebar to start editing.

How to See Your Changes

  1. Edit content in any section (Company Info, Hero, Services, etc.)
  2. Click "Save" button at the bottom of each section
  3. Click "Refresh Preview" button at the top to open/refresh the website
  4. Your changes will appear instantly on the website!

Tip: Keep both the CMS and website tabs open side-by-side to see changes in real-time.
