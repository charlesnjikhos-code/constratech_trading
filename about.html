About Constratech Trading

Building Excellence Since 2015

Who We Are

Constratech Trading is a fully registered and certified construction company operating in Malawi. Established in 2015, we have grown to become a trusted name in the construction industry, delivering high-quality projects across various sectors.

We are registered with the Registrar of Companies, the National Construction Industry Council of Malawi (NCIC), the Malawi Revenue Authority, the National Water Resources Authority Ministry, PPDA, and the Ministry of Trade.

Our NCIC registration qualifies us for projects up to MK 500 million in both Civil and Building categories, demonstrating our capacity and expertise to handle substantial construction projects.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction and solar power solutions that exceed client expectations through innovation, quality craftsmanship, and sustainable practices. We are committed to building lasting relationships and contributing to Malawi's infrastructure development.

Our Vision

To be the leading construction and renewable energy solutions provider in Malawi, recognized for excellence, innovation, and sustainable development. We aspire to set industry standards and contribute significantly to the nation's economic growth.
